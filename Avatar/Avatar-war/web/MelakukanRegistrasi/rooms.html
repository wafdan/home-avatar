room information

Check in, relax and unwind in one of our 465 spacious and comfortable hotel rooms. The Spons Hotel is recently renovated, making us prouder than ever before of our hotel and the service we offer our guests.

Experience tells us that a good night's sleep is always the highest priority, especially when you're far from home. This is why all our guest rooms have at least one Spons Sweet Sleeper Bed, so you can be sure you'll sleep beautifully. The bed has a luxurious mattress, crisp white cotton sheets, soft goose feather and down pillows - and an entirely new level of comfort.

Our hotel rooms are generously sized - actually, they are among the largest in Bandung. All the rooms are recently renovated; they are inviting and have modern technology, making the Spons Hotel a place where you can both work and relax. All of our public areas, such as the lobby, restaurant, bar and Fitness Center, are designed to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Every room has at least one Sweet Sleeper Bed, a bed that has been chosen with greatest care and which can be found in all Spons hotels, all around the world.

After a day of friends, family, or business, there is one more thing to savor at the Spons Hotel: the welcome of our guest rooms, which are completely smoke-free.
